## What's blocking new duplicates from being inserted

| Layer | Mechanism |
|---|---|
| Writer-side | `sidebar-pipeline.js::upsertDomainSales` does soft pre-insert dedup (Stage 1 doc# + Stage 2 price/date window) |
| Schema-side | `ux_sales_transactions_dedup_live` partial UNIQUE on `dedup_natural_key` WHERE `transaction_state='live'` — physically rejects collisions with SQLSTATE 23505 |
| Cron-side | `sales_dedup_tick()` every 15 min catches anything that slips past (bulk-load, race, NULL-then-populated edge cases) |
| Pre-existing | `(property_id, sale_date, sold_price)` exact-match UNIQUE was already in place on both domains |
