The core idea: the broken links in a property's chain are not defects to hide — they are the prospecting opportunities to surface.

A property with an unresolved true owner, a name-only ownership timeline, and no known developer is exactly the research target worth pursuing. The system already computes every one of these gaps (the next-best-research and ownership-research queues hold 67,000+ rows). This flow re-sequences the presentation so each phase pushes that intelligence forward instead of dead-ending in a tab. The two blue payoff phases — de-anonymizing ownership to the developer, and converging on the next prospecting action — are where the value lands.
