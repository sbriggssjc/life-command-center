qa(15): fix Research page widgets being destroyed by parent render

Research page rendered as just "Research · 0 tasks · No research
tasks match this filter" even though the LLC research queue had
1,200+ items and the Agency Drift queue had hundreds of rows.
Both widget renders were wired (Item #2 Phase B on 2026-05-17,
Fresh audit A-5 on 2026-05-18) but produced no visible output.

Root cause: renderResearchPage (ops.js) called the widget renders
AT THE TOP. They each prepended a <div> into el via
`parentEl.insertBefore(widget, parentEl.firstChild)`. Then the
function continued building the queue-list `html` string and
finished with `el.innerHTML = html` — which replaced every child
of el, wiping out the just-rendered widgets.

No console error: the widgets WERE rendering, the parent function
destroyed them on the next line. The bug was a render-order issue,
not a missing function.

Fix: restructure renderResearchPage to set el.innerHTML to two
wrapper divs FIRST — an empty .lcc-research-widgets placeholder
and a .lcc-research-queue containing the queue-list html — then
call the widget renders, passing the .lcc-research-widgets wrapper
so they prepend into it without being overwritten by subsequent
re-renders.

After:
  • LLC Research Queue widget renders at the top of the page,
    listing the top 15 queued LLCs with Open SoS → / Mark found /
    etc.
  • Agency Drift widget renders below it, showing agency-name
    mismatches between property + lease records.
  • The original research tasks list (0 tasks today) renders
    below both widgets.
