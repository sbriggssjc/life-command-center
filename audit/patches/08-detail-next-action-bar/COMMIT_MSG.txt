audit(item-8/phase-a): sticky next-action bar on detail.js

Companion to the Item #6 completeness rail:
  • Completeness rail (top)    — what is MISSING for this property
  • Next-action bar  (bottom)  — what to DO FIRST right now

For each property opened in the unified detail panel, fetches the single
top-ranked open gap from v_next_best_action (already live on dia + gov)
and renders it as a sticky bar pinned to the bottom of the panel.

The bar shows:
  • Severity chip (CRIT/HIGH/MED/LOW, color-coded)
  • Suggested action text (from view.suggested_action)
  • Meta line: value estimate · target tab
  • Primary "Take action →" CTA
  • Border-top stripe color-coded by severity

Click anywhere on the bar (or the CTA button) → switches to the tab where
the action lives:
  missing_recorded_owner   → Ownership & CRM
  llc_research_pending     → Ownership & CRM
  lease_tenant_drift       → Rent Roll
  orphan_sale_owner        → Deal History
  stale_active_listing     → Overview
  cms_chain_drift:*        → Operations

Auto-hides when the property has no open gap.
