Three fixes in this patch:

1. sf_comps_staging writer rewrite
   Real schema: street/sold_price/sold_date/building_sf/source_system/
   process_status/raw_row.
   Old writer sent: address/sale_price/sale_date/buyer_name/seller_name/
   square_feet/sync_status (every column wrong, every POST 400'd
   with PGRST204 "could not find the 'address' column").
   Rewritten the column map; buyer + seller stash in raw_row jsonb.
   Label: 'autoStageGovComp'.

2. gov leases dateless-active skip
   The gov_reject_dateless_active_lease trigger correctly rejects
   active leases with both dates NULL. Writer now short-circuits
   when both dates are missing — log + continue. Label on the
   genuine POST: 'upsertGovernmentLeases:insert'.

3. rpc/lcc_record_listing_check
   The auto-scrape path writes check_result='inferred_active' when
   the timer expires without sale evidence. The CHECK only allowed
   6 values. Expanded on both dia + gov via MCP. Plus labeled 3 RPC
   call sites: autoScrapeListings:recordCheck,
   availabilityPromotionSweep:recordCheck,
   entitiesHandler:recordListingCheck.
