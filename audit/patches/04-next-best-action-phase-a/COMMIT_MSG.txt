audit(item-4/a): propagation gap views + v_next_best_action core (dia)

Phase A of audit item #4 (Phase B = gov mirror + LCC Opps view + backend
endpoint + Home rail UI, deferred to next session).

Closes the dia side of audit findings B-1 (unified next-best-action queue),
B-3 (value-weighted sort), and lays the foundation for B-13 (replaces the
wrong-table Home Research pulse-card once the UI lands).

The audit identified that the LCC has rich gap-tracking primitives but
they live in three separate worlds that don't talk to each other:
  • Strategic priority scoring (briefing-data.js)
  • Property-research queues (gov.js / dialysis.js per-screen heuristics)
  • Data-quality / provenance views (ops.js only)

This commit creates the SQL foundation that unifies them on the dia side.
Three new propagation-gap views detect cross-table denormalization drift
(audit findings A-7 / A-13 / discovered surface), and v_next_best_action
UNIONs every known gap source into a single ranked queue.
