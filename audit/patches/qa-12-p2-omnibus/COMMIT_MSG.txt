qa(12): P2 omnibus — casing, calendar, FAB, header

Bundle of small fixes from the 2026-05-18 QA pass. Each is a few
lines on its own; together they remove the most visible "unfinished
dashboard" papercuts.

1. Address direction-suffix canonicalization (DATA FIX, both DBs)
   - New IMMUTABLE helper public.canonicalize_address_directions(text)
     that upper-cases USPS direction suffixes (Se→SE, Sw→SW, Ne→NE,
     Nw→NW) on a whole-word match.
   - One-shot backfill on properties.address: gov 710 rows, dia 450
     rows. Verified property 3198 now stores "1200 New Jersey Ave SE".
   - BEFORE INSERT/UPDATE trigger keeps future writes canonical.

2. AI Copilot FAB accessibility
   - index.html: added aria-label="Open AI Copilot" to #copilotFab.
     The visible SVG icon was already present; only the screen-reader
     label was missing.

3. Calendar zero-duration events
   - app.js renderCalendarFull: events with start_time === end_time
     are Outlook tasks ingested as zero-duration calendar entries.
     They were rendering as "5:40 AM – 5:40 AM Essentia to Brokers".
     Now render as "Task @ 5:40 AM".

4. Detail panel header — duplicated city
   - detail.js (two sites: loading-state header at line ~117 and
     fully-hydrated header at line ~480). The page_title often
     embeds the city ("1200 New Jersey Ave SE – Washington, DC")
     and the subtitle was rendering "Washington, DC" again right
     below it. The subtitle is now suppressed when its content is
     a substring of the title.

5. Data Quality duplicate-candidate cluster cleanup
   - ops.js renderDataQualityPage:
     a. Suppresses parse-debris clusters where canonical_name is
        null AND every member name is a 2-letter state code (these
        were rendering as "Unnamed · 4 matches · CO · CO · CO · CO").
     b. Title-cases the cluster label so "townebank · 3 matches"
        renders as "Townebank · 3 matches" (matching the
        Title-cased children listed below).
