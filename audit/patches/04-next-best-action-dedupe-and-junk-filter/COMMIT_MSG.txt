audit(item-4/v3.2): dedupe + junk filter on missing_recorded_owner

Closes Discovery #4 (junk property records) + Discovery #5 (duplicate
property records at same address) — both surfaced by the v3 NOI/cap
valuation fix exposing them at the top of the ranked queue.

Changes (dia + gov, applied via Supabase MCP at 2026-05-17)
-----------------------------------------------------------

Both v_next_best_action.missing_recorded_owner branches rewritten to:
  • Filter junk addresses: require NOT NULL, ≥8 chars after trim, start
    with a digit, not pure digits + whitespace, not start with
    "property #" placeholder.
  • Dedupe by (lower(trim(address)), lower(trim(city)), state): keep
    smallest property_id per partition. Surface dup_count > 1 inline
    in gap_label as "[N dup records]" so Scott sees duplicates at a
    glance, and suggested_action prompts consolidation.

Impact
------

Dia: missing_recorded_owner rows 13,338 → 10,115 (−3,223 junk + dedupe).
Gov: top of queue now shows "6120 S. Yale Ave., Ste. 300 [7 dup records]"
inline instead of 7 separate ranks. All top entries are real street
addresses.

Edge cases remaining (deferred):
  • "2 locations" still passes (starts with "2", >8 chars). Would need
    a street-suffix predicate to catch.
  • Subtle city/state variations leave a few same-address records in
    separate partition groups.
