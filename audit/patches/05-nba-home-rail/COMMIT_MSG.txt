audit(item-4/phase-c): Next Best Action rail on Home tab

User-visible payoff for the entire Item #4 build. Renders the cross-domain
prioritized broker queue (dia + gov v_next_best_action) at the top of the
Home page so Scott opens the app and immediately sees the highest-value
gaps to close.

Widget placement: directly under the 4 Home stat cards, above Weather.
Top 10 ranked rows merged globally by gap_value DESC across both domains.
Each row shows rank, severity chip, domain tag, gap_label (with the
[N dup records] annotation from v3.2 when present), suggested action,
and value estimate (NOI ÷ cap_rate from v3). Left-border stripe is
color-coded by severity. Clicking a row opens openUnifiedDetail(db,
{ property_id }) for the underlying record.

Controls:
  • Domain switch (All / Dialysis / Government), persisted in
    localStorage['lcc.nba.domain'].
  • Manual refresh button.
  • Auto-refresh on the existing 5-minute interval and on tab
    visibilitychange.

Endpoint:
  GET /api/admin?_route=next-best-action&domain={both|dia|gov}&limit=15
  (handleNextBestAction, shipped Phase B-2)
